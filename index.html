Mini Rummy is a simplified version of the classic card game Rummy, designed for quick and fun gameplay. The objective of the game is to be the first player to get rid of all your cards by forming valid sets and runs.

In Mini Rummy, players are dealt a hand of 10 cards and take turns drawing and discarding cards to create sets (three or four cards of the same rank e.g. 7, 7, 7) and runs (three or more consecutive cards of the same suit e.g. 4, 5, 6, 7, 8 of hearts). The first player to successfully form valid combinations and discard all their cards wins the game.

Mini Rummy is perfect for casual gaming sessions, offering a fast-paced and enjoyable experience for players of all ages.
